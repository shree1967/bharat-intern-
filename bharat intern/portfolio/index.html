Project 2

Developed a CAD system for melanoma detection in dermoscopy images, focusing on distinctive lesion border structures. We segmented skin lesions using a novel active contours-based method, utilizing the Kullback-Leibler divergence to fit a curve to lesion boundaries. Post-segmentation, the lesion periphery was extracted, and features based on local binary patterns were used for melanoma detection. Validation with PH2 and ISIC datasets showed that our segmentation method closely mimics ground truth data, and significant melanoma characteristics are primarily found at the lesion periphery.
